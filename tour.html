    The Winona State University Landscape Arboretum offers a wide variety of tours ranging from majestic mature trees that dot the campus to collections of grasses and flowers that attract birds and migrating butterflies. Visitors can choose from our prearranged tour listings carefully curated by our expert staff to provide patrons with an experience highlighting specific specimens that best showcase the category selection.


    We here at WSU are firm believers that every day is a great day to learn something new. As such, we invite visitors to tour the picturesque campus and engage with our self-guided tours, aided with useful placards that define the species of plant and a QR code that allows visitors to learn more through engaging plant-specific site pages. Visitors can view locations on campus with our plant map, which will provide pin locations of where to search for a specific plant.


    To begin your tour, please select from one of the pre-listed options and then hit “Load Tour.”
